# Design System Strategy: The Tactile Sanctuary (Updated)

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
The Creative North Star for this design system is **"The Tactile Sanctuary."** 

Digital wellness shouldn't feel like a digital prison; it should feel like a retreat. This system avoids the clinical "productivity" look in favor of an organic, grounded aesthetic. We use soft shadows, large touch targets, and earthen tones to make the interface feel less like a screen and more like a physical object.

## 2. Digital Sanctuary Asset Pack

### The Guardian: Lumina
- **Visual Description:** A minimalist, ethereal cloud-like creature.
- **Rule:** Strictly follows the 'No-Line' rule.
- **States:** 5 levels of glow intensity.
  - Level 0: Rest (faint visibility).
  - Level 4: Active Presence (pulsing radiance).

### The Habitat: Progressive Resort Elements
1. **Moss Garden:** Base habitat layer. Color: Sage Green (`#4C6545`). Represents the foundation of calmness.
2. **Stone Path:** Secondary layer. Color: Bone (`#FAF9F5`). Represents the intentional journey.
3. **Ethereal Waterfall:** Final progression layer. Represents the flow of time and digital cleansing.

## 3. Design Tokens (Aether Motion Lab Palette)

### Colors
- **Primary (Sage Green):** `#8A9A5B` / `#4C6545` — Used for nature elements and primary actions.
- **Surface (Bone):** `#FAF9F6` / `#FAF9F5` — The primary background and tactile path elements.
- **On-Surface (Charcoal):** `#2E342D` — Balanced contrast for readability.

### Rules & Principles
- **The 'No-Line' Rule:** No hard outlines or borders. Depth and separation are achieved through soft shadows, gradients, and overlapping organic shapes.
- **Aether Motion:** All movements must be weighted and fluid (`cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)`).

## 4. Typography (Manrope)
- **Headlines:** Semi-bold, tight tracking.
- **Body:** Regular weight, generous line-height.

## 5. Shape & Geometry
- **Corner Radius:** Full Roundness (`9999px`) where applicable.
- **Organic Forms:** Preference for asymmetrical, pebble-like shapes over rigid squares.
